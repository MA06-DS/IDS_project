## Project Overview

A machine-learning–powered system designed to **predict future stock prices** for the **Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX)** using historical data and advanced time-series forecasting techniques.

---

## Key Points

### ► Problem Addressed

* The stock market is inherently **volatile and uncertain**.
* Relying on manual judgment or intuition often leads to losses.
* There is a strong need for **data-driven forecasting** to support better decision-making.

### ► Why It Matters

* Enables investors to make **informed buy/sell decisions**.
* Builds **confidence** and promotes **market stability**.
* Helps institutional investors refine and optimize **portfolio strategies**.

### ► Role of Machine Learning

* The model learns from **historical price behavior**.
* Utilizes **LSTM-based time-series forecasting**.
* Predicts the **next day’s stock price** rather than providing generic signals.

### ► Dataset Used

* **KSE-100 Index** historical price data
* Sources: **Yahoo Finance**, **PSX**, **Kaggle**
* Includes:

  * Date, Open, High, Low, Close
  * Adjusted Close
  * Volume

### ► Model Inputs (Features)

* Open, High, Low, Close, Volume
* Technical indicators (e.g., Moving Averages)

### ► Model Output (Target)

* **Next day’s Adjusted Close price**

---

## Outcome

The developed model:

* Provides **reasonably accurate stock price forecasts**
* Encourages **data-driven investment strategies**
* Enhances PSX’s overall **accessibility, transparency, and efficiency**
